Robust Quest


To become a Robust Tamer, u need lv 201 first. But u can farm all drops before ur reaches 201.
When u are ready need to talk with Jijimon in Babaland or Mud Village.
Robust Quest have 5 parts:

Robust Quest 1

x30 Icedevimon's Drops
x30 WereGarurumon's Drops
x30 Meteormon's Drops

Robust Quest 2

x30 Paildramon's Drops
x30 Cyberdramon's Drops
x30 Groundramon's Drops

Robust Quest 3

x20 Etemon's Drops
x20 WaruMonzaemon's Drops
x20 Monzaemon's Drops

Robust Quest 4

x20 Phantomon's Drops
x20 Azuramon's Drops
x20 Cerberusmon's Drops

Robust Quest 5

x5 SaberLeomon's Drops
x5 Breakdramon's Drops
And need go Lab3 map and get
x5 Anubismon's Drops
x5 Diaboromon's Drops
Sky 3 Quest

When u are in Sky, can farm the rare item "Unstable Data Fragments".
This item can be drop from any Digimon in Sky1/2, you requered to collect as many 25 pieces per entry.
If have 25 drops, go talk with Jijimon in the topside of Sky2
