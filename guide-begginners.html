    The Digis i recommend capture are:


    Data Digimon

    Candlemon

    Dynasmon its the best Mega without card evolution.
    He have critical traits, and the mega scaled 4 str.
    The other opcion its Gabumon, but need 2 card evo for make MetalGarurumon X.

    Later u can change ur Main Data digi to MetalGaruruX or Baihumon.

    Vaccine Digimon

    Lopmon

    When capturing it we will have it as Cherubimon Evil, when reaching level 51 we can finish a quest to change the evolution line to Cherubimon Good.

    Agumon

    The bad of Agu compared to Lopmon is that you need the card evo of BlackTyrannomon, MasterTyrannomon and Wargreymon X.
    Because Wargreymon normal its a very very bad digi.

    Later u can change ur Main Vaccine digi to OmegaX or AO.

    Virus Digimon

    D.Devimon (Myotismon Line)

    If you do not go for Dynasmon, the mega of D.Devimon (Myotismon) has AOE in Skill F3, to farm tamer lv is very useful. Later u can make MalomMyotismon, and get your first Digimon Mega with Combo Trait.

    Salamon (Lilithmon)

    The best Virus digimon without Combo Trait in my opinion its Lilithmon, she have good stats and decents traits. The bad its u need 2 card evo.

    Later u can change ur Main VI digi to Zhuqiamon.

    In my opinion the best digi to farm, if it possible have 2 better. For more information about Holy Beast click here

    In this map u can found Agumon lv 33, but u need care to fight to B not V. The Digimons Enemy have ranks, for more info about this go check FAQ.

    Now its time to use the Mud Frigimon Teleport

    You are in Mud Frigimon village, take the right teleport.

    Nearby portal u can find Cherrymon lv 73, stay here to lv 41.
    Remember upgrade ur stats, all points in STR

    Now u can decided vs what digi u go lv up. The only difference of these 3 is the attribute.

    I pref the aeroveedra because u are in Square World S, only need use the top teleport to go Square World.

    Back to MudFrigimon Village to buy cards. This increased ur damage.
